CosmicCards Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

CosmicCards ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, which lets you read daily horoscopes for your zodiac sign and create and share astrology cards. CosmicCards is provided for entertainment purposes only.

Information We Collect

Content You Create

  • Your sign, readings, and preferences: The zodiac sign you select, the gradients and fonts you choose, and the cards you save are stored locally on your device. We do not receive a copy of them. We do not ask for your exact birth date or birth time — only the sign you choose.

Anonymous Usage Analytics

To understand which features are useful and to improve the app, we collect a small amount of anonymous usage information, such as:

  • App opens, card shares, and feature interactions (for example, choosing a sign or saving a card)
  • Your app version and device platform (iOS or Android)

These events are associated only with a random identifier generated on your device — never with your name, email, or any personal identity. This information is used solely for analytics and app functionality. It is not linked to you and is not used to track you across other apps or websites.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT require account creation, and we do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do NOT collect your birth date, birth time, or location
  • We do NOT collect your advertising identifier (IDFA), and we do NOT use it for advertising
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

Data Storage

The sign, readings, and preferences you keep stay on your device. The anonymous usage events described above are transmitted to our own analytics service to help us improve the app. Because they contain no personal information and use only a random identifier, they cannot be tied back to your identity.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

  • CosmicCards does not display third-party advertisements and does not include advertising SDKs.
  • We use our own first-party, anonymous analytics only. We do not sell or share your information.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Used solely for app distribution.

Entertainment Only

Horoscopes and zodiac content in CosmicCards are provided for entertainment and self-reflection purposes only. They are not a substitute for professional advice of any kind.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: View your saved cards within the app.
  • Delete: Remove saved cards in the app, or uninstall the app to remove all local data.
  • Because the analytics we collect are anonymous and not linked to you, they cannot be associated with or traced back to you.

Children's Privacy

CosmicCards is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
