StreakMaster Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Introduction

StreakMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Habit Data: Habit names, completion records, and streak counts
  • Goals: Your daily habit targets and schedules
  • Settings: Your preferred themes, notification preferences

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT require account creation
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT store your personal data on our servers. Your data remains private and under your control.

Third-Party Services

StreakMaster is a completely free app with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Used solely for app distribution
  • We do NOT use any analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do NOT process any payments — the app is 100% free

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Control: Manage notifications and preferences in Settings

Children's Privacy

StreakMaster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
