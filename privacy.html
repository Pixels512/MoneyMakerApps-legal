Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how FastTrack, AquaLog, and StreakMaster ("we", "our", or "us") collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile applications (the "Apps"). All our Apps are 100% free with no subscriptions or in-app purchases.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Settings: Your preferred fasting schedule, water intake goals, and app preferences
  • Health Data: Fasting start/end times, water consumption records, weight tracking data (if you choose to enter it)
  • User Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, theme choices

Automatically Collected Information

  • None. We do not collect any data automatically. No analytics, no crash reporting, no device fingerprinting.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT require account creation
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT access your camera, microphone, or contacts
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide App Functionality: Track your fasting periods, water intake, habits, and progress
  • Personalize Experience: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Send Notifications: Remind you about fasting times, hydration goals, or habit check-ins (only if you enable notifications)

Data Storage

Local Storage

  • All your health data (fasting times, water intake, weight) is stored locally on your device
  • We do NOT store your health data on our servers
  • Your data remains private and under your control

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If you enable iCloud sync (iOS) or Google Drive backup (Android), your data may be backed up to Apple or Google's servers
  • This is controlled by your device settings, not by us
  • We do not have access to your cloud-synced data

Data Sharing

We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our Apps are completely free and do not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or payment processing.

  • App Distribution: Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android) are used solely for app distribution
  • We do NOT use analytics SDKs
  • We do NOT use advertising networks
  • We do NOT process payments — all features are free

Your Rights

  • Access Your Data:All your data is stored locally on your device and can be viewed anytime
  • Delete Your Data: Go to Settings → Clear All Data
  • Export Your Data: Settings → Export Data (available to all users for free)

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

GDPR Compliance (EU): You have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port your data.

CCPA Compliance (California): You can request information we collect and opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data).
